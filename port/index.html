About me

Student of Interactive Product Design and Virtual Reality

Hello, I am Julieta Beltrame, as a 22-year-old studying interactive product design and virtual reality, I am passionate about exploring the latest technologies and using my skills to create immersive experiences. I am an enthusiastic learner who enjoys keeping up with industry trends and advancements, and I thrive in collaborative environments where I can work with others to bring ideas to life. With experience in software such as Blender, Photoshop, Illustrator, and web design tools, I am adept at creating visually appealing and user-friendly designs. Additionally, I am familiar with agile methodologies and am comfortable working in fast-paced, iterative environments. I am eager to contribute my talents to any project and help drive innovation in the field of design.

Services

With my diverse skill set and expertise in cutting-edge design technologies, I am able to deliver high-quality solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. With a focus on quality, creativity, and attention to detail, I am committed to exceeding your expectations and delivering designs that make a lasting impression.
